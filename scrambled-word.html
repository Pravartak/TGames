About the game

Dynamic Word Selection: At the beginning of each round, the game dynamically selects a random word from an extensive word bank.
You have to Scramble name of animal,bird,places and fruit.

 Real-Time Scrambling: The chosen word is instantly transformed into a captivating, scrambled version, challenging players to unscramble it.

 Interactive User Input: An intuitive input field empowers players to submit their best guess, creating a sense of participation and excitement.

 Immediate Validation: In real-time, the game evaluates the player's answer, offering instant feedback that keeps the momentum alive.

 Constructive Feedback: Constructive feedback, in the form of "Correct!" or "Try again!", guides players toward mastery and promotes a growth mindset.

 Seamless Transition: After each round, the game seamlessly transitions to the next word, ensuring a continuous and enjoyable experience.

 Challenging Rounds: Players can enjoy multiple rounds of word scrambling, with each word increasing in complexity, providing an escalating challenge.

 Final Score Showcase: At the end of the game, the player's final score is prominently displayed, allowing them to measure their success and set new goals.

 Change word Option: For those who things it's hard just try other words!
